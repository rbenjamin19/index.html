Crystals Collector

You'll be given a desired number at the start of the game
There are four crystals below. Clicking each crystal will add a specific amount of points to your score.
If you match your score to the desired score you win. If your score is greater than the desired score you lose.
The values of each crystal will change every round.
The value of each crystal will stay consistent throughout each round and each crystals value will be hidden.
